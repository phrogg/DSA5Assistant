The DSA Assistant is an assistant for you while playing "The Dark Eye" a german alteration to Pen and Paper.

Functions:
 - Money Manager
 - Combat Manager
 - Dice Roller
 - GameMaster INI Manager

Please note: Until now the whole app is just in german. I'm currently working on the english version.
